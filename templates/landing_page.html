Customer Onboarding

Save Drafts to carry over progress through sessions as you receive information from your customers, and track progress of the ticket right from the Status Table to check stage, errors, and completion times. Update Information/Documents at any stage without having to restart your work. Recieve E-mail Updates whenever the ticket is updated.

Product Requisition

Move an O2C request along it's lifecycle and automatically fire e-mails to responsible parties with the required information and Comments/Remarks.

Visit Tracking

Plan Visits and view your progress as you go through your monthly visiting goals. Managers can assign visits for high priority cases.

SLA Monitoring and Visualization

Managers/Admins can track and view Summaries and Visualizations to identify problems in the pipeline and monitor progress.

Generate Monthly Visit Reports

Download KAM-wise monthly reports for managers, and Segment-wise monthly reports for Segment Leaders.
